Built for centers

Everything a manager
actually needs.

From collecting availability to copying the week's hours — one tool handles the whole workflow.

🔗
Shareable availability form

Open the availability form and share the link with your staff. Employees pick their available days and set their hours — submissions land in your dashboard automatically.

🔐
PIN-based staff access

Each staff member gets a unique PIN. No accounts or passwords needed — employees just enter their PIN to access the form and submit their availability.

🗓️
Google Calendar integration

Connect your center's Google Calendar once. Cale reads your student session data and uses it to inform every schedule it generates.

⚡
One-click schedule generation

Once availability is in, hit "Build Schedule." Cale cross-references staff hours and student demand to generate a complete, visual weekly schedule.

📋
Copy-ready weekly hours

The finished schedule formats each employee's hours for the week and puts them on your clipboard. Paste into a text, email, or group chat — done.

🏢
Multi-center support

Running more than one location? Each center gets its own isolated workspace, employee roster, and schedule — all managed from one place.

The workflow

From availability
to send-ready
in minutes.

1

Staff submit availability

Manager shares the form link. Staff enter their PIN, check their available days, and set their hours for the week.

2

Manager reviews the dashboard

See who's submitted, confirm Google Calendar is connected, and track submission count in real time. When ready, build the schedule.

3

Schedule generated, hours copied

Cale generates a visual timeline with each employee's shifts per day. Hit "Copy to Clipboard" and paste the formatted hours wherever you send them.
